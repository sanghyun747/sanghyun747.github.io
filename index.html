About

Sanghyun Park is an undergraduate student in Biomedical Engineering at Yonsei University, with interests in biosignal analysis, biomedical AI, and building software that turns research workflows into usable tools. He is currently an undergraduate researcher in Professor Ki-Jae Hong's lab, where he works on actigraphy-based biosignal data analysis and depression-related classification modeling.

His background began in a science-focused high school track, where he studied life sciences and gained early experience writing a student research paper. Since entering university, he has continued to work at the intersection of research and product development, connecting data preprocessing, model evaluation, and user-facing software.

He participated as a developer in an award-winning Yonsei Ideathon team and has worked with Verdigris Team 3 on source-grounded AI product interfaces and verification-centered development workflows.
